Problem / Market Opportunity

🔴 The Problem

Players want cooperative dungeon experiences, but most titles in the genre lack:

  • True gameplay depth (classes, systems, puzzles)
  • Proximity voice as a game mechanic
  • Sufficient procedural variability
  • Emergent AI that reacts to player behavior

🟢 The Opportunity

$100M+ Lethal Company revenue
3M+ Dark and Darker copies sold
∞ Viral potential (voice clips)

Explosive growth of co-op games on Steam. Players are actively searching for new titles in this segment.
